The second annual report on the Paulding County Experiment Farm, an
Ohio Agricultural Experiment Station research station established in
the county to study farming methods suited to its heavy, reclaimed
Black Swamp soils. Given the numbering, likely covers 1912, published
around 1913. Not reproduced here — bibliographic entry only. See also
the
