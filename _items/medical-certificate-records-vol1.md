A name index to Paulding County Probate Court medical practice
certificates: physicians' diploma certificates (1865-1948), legal
practitioner certificates (1896), examination certificates
(1896-1939), and midwives' certificates issued by the probate judge
(1938-1945) — the latter apparently the only formal midwife licensing
mechanism the county used, since no separate midwife examination
records were found registered. A research finding aid rather than
county-history narrative — see the full PDF for the complete index.
