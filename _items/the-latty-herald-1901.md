A slice-of-life local news column from Latty, Ohio's weekly paper:
families moving between farms, visits to and from Dague, Broughton,
Haviland, and Hicksville, a new baby at the Michaels/Albright home, an
oyster supper by the ladies of the M.E. church on election night, and
notes on residents' comings and goings — the kind of routine
small-town reporting that rarely survives outside a paper's own back
issues.
