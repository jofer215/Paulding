Catalog listing for a broad narrative history of northwest Ohio's
development, covering the region from the first European exploration of the
Maumee River forward. Paulding County sits within this larger regional
story — the Great Black Swamp, canal-era commerce, and settlement patterns
described elsewhere in this archive all connect to the wider Maumee Valley
context this book covers.

Useful as regional background reading rather than a Paulding-specific
source; cite it for context, not county-specific claims, unless you've
confirmed what it says about the county directly.
