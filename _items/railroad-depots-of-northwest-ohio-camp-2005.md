A photograph-driven history of railroad depots across northwest Ohio,
by Mark J. Camp — a University of Toledo geology professor, longtime
railroad historian, and national director of the Railroad Station
Historical Society. Published by Arcadia Publishing as part of their
regional "Images of Rail" series.

This archive already has several depot and railroad-scene photographs
of its own — see
[Crowd at the Antwerp Depot]({{ "/archive/antwerp-depot-crowd/" | relative_url }}),
[Railroad Depot Scene, Paulding]({{ "/archive/railroad-depot-winters-bros/" | relative_url }}),
and the
[Jackson Street Bridge, 1921]({{ "/archive/jackson-street-bridge-1921/" | relative_url }}).
Camp's book likely covers the same Paulding County depots (and others
across the region) in more depth; not yet confirmed which specific
towns it includes.
