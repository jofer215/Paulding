This 1992 compilation reprints pages 179-239 of the 1882
*Historical Hand-Atlas*, covering both Everett A. Budd's county history
essay — already transcribed in this archive as
[History of Paulding County, Ohio (Historical Hand-Atlas, 1882)]({{ "/archive/historical-hand-atlas-1882-paulding-county-history/" | relative_url }}),
which notes the original "continues beyond this excerpt" — and
township and personal histories of Paulding County that haven't been
transcribed anywhere in this archive yet.

Not reproduced here (surfaced via Anna's Archive, a shadow-library
site), but flagged as a strong candidate for a future `document` entry
covering the township/personal-histories continuation specifically.
