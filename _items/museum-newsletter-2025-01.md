Covers the 2024 Festival of Trees (nearly 3,000 attendees), the new Crane
Township bicentennial flag, and a nationally ranked bur oak tree on Road
180 believed to predate the clearing of the Great Black Swamp.

Also includes two short historical reprints, transcribed together as
["Paulding County Newspaper Reminiscences"]({{ "/archive/paulding-county-newspaper-reminiscences/" | relative_url }}):
a tribute to early women settlers from an 1888 address at Antwerp's
Pioneer Day, and a 1919 "Do You Remember" column on the county's early
county seats and infrastructure.

See the full PDF for the 2025 event schedule and a list of newest museum
acquisitions.
