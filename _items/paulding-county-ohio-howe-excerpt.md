A locally-produced booklet isolating just the Paulding County chapter
of Henry Howe's *Historical Collections of Ohio* — the full statewide
volume is linked at
[Historical Collections of Ohio, Vol. II]({{ "/archive/howes-historical-collections-of-ohio-1907/" | relative_url }}).
The chapter begins around page 374 of the original and covers county
geography and streams, and includes Howe's "Sketch of John Paulding" —
the county's namesake, one of the three militiamen who captured Major
André during the Revolutionary War.

See the full PDF for the complete chapter text; not fully transcribed
here yet.
