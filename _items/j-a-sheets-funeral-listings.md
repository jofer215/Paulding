A listing of funerals held by undertaker J.A. Sheets of Latty, Ohio,
April 1896 - August 1897 — names, ages, and dates of death, including
a striking number of infant deaths. This archive's
[unattributed county history typescript]({{ "/archive/paulding-county-early-history-typescript/" | relative_url }})
separately notes that the county's first undertaker was a Mr. Schilb of
Antwerp. A research finding aid rather than county-history narrative —
see the full PDF for the complete listing.
