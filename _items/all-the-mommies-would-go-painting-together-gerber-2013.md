A scrapbook-style history of "the art of art" in Paulding County — a
group of local women who painted together — compiled by Alice L.
Gerber. Per contemporary coverage in the *Paulding County Progress*,
Gerber described it as "their story... put together like a scrapbook,
but without the glue and bumps of embellishments," and copies were
donated to the Paulding Carnegie Library branches and the John Paulding
Historical Society. Hardbound, originally sold for $15.
