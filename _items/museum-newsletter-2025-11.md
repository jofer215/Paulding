Covers the day-by-day schedule for the 2025 Festival of Trees ("Sparkle &
Shine"), the museum's upcoming role hosting the Ohio Local History
Alliance Region 1 meeting (where member Carol Razo would be recognized
for her book on Crane Township), a visit from Dr. Resend Dereck Ross —
descendant of museum founder Dr. George Phillips — and results from the
2025 ATHS Vintage Truck Show. Also includes a recipe for Iris' Sour
Dough Cookies, from a longtime Paulding bakery.
