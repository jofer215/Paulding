This issue announces the organization's rebranding from John Paulding
Historical Society to Paulding County History Museum — explicitly to
reduce confusion with the county's Revolutionary War namesake, since (as
the newsletter notes) John Paulding "never set foot in Ohio, and he never
owned land here." It also covers the Festival of Trees, a Canal Society
of Indiana tour of the county's canal sites, the 23rd annual ATHS Vintage
Truck Show, and an Ohio Historical Marker awarded for New Rochester, the
county's first county seat.

A substantial historical reprint has been transcribed as its own entry in
this archive:

- ["A Visit to Cecil in 1886"]({{ "/archive/a-visit-to-cecil-in-1886/" | relative_url }}) — "Cecil Chimes," from the *Paulding Democrat*, April 8, 1886
