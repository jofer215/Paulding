A statutory delinquent-tax-sale notice from the Paulding Independent,
listing landowners with unpaid taxes by town (Andrews, Newburg,
Melrose, Junction, and Paulding, among others) and lot number, as
required by law before a tax sale. More property/economic history than
genealogical record proper, but still primarily a name-and-lot list
rather than a narrative — see the full PDF for the complete notice.
