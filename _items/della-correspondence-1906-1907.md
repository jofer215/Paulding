A set of personal letters from 1906-1907 discussing an unwanted pregnancy
and abortion in the Paulding, Ohio area. Identifying names were removed by
the original compiler before publication.

This is sensitive personal material rather than an institutional or
public record — included here as part of the county's social history, not
as a genealogical record of any named individual.
