On the evening of Nov. 13, 1960, 14-year-old Nancy Eagleson was abducted
and murdered while walking home with her younger sister on Flatrock
Drive in Paulding. Her killing was never solved, and the case remains
open — including a 2022 exhumation that recovered new physical
evidence.

This entry is a running list of sources covering the case, not a
transcription or summary of any of them. It will grow as more turn up;
what's here so far is a small fraction of the coverage this case has
gotten over the decades.
