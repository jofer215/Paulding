The full roster of Company B, 2nd Ohio Infantry, as of its July 24,
1917 muster — Paulding County's World War I company, led by Capt.
Raymond C. Leslie — plus a separate list of young men from Payne,
Harrison, and Benton Townships who enlisted elsewhere. This is the
World War I counterpart to the Civil War-era Co. G roster already
recorded in this archive's
[unattributed county history typescript]({{ "/archive/paulding-county-early-history-typescript/" | relative_url }})
(headquartered at Antwerp, under Capt. John S. Snook, part of the 14th
Ohio Volunteer Infantry).
