An early photograph of the Paulding County Courthouse (completed 1888,
per this archive's [Sheriff's Office history]({{ "/archive/history-of-paulding-county-sheriffs-office/" | relative_url }})),
hand-labeled "Court House Paulding Co." on the negative or print. The
courthouse square bandstand is visible at left — the same bandstand
described in later museum newsletters as the site of summer band
concerts. Early automobiles alongside horse-drawn vehicles suggest a date
in the 1910s. Compare with the [later courthouse postcard]({{ "/archive/paulding-courthouse-postcard-1940s/" | relative_url }})
in this archive, taken decades afterward.
