The third annual report on the Paulding County Experiment Farm, covering
the 1913 growing season and published in 1914 by the Ohio Agricultural
Experiment Station. Part of a series studying farming methods suited to
Paulding County's heavy, reclaimed Black Swamp soils. Not reproduced
here — bibliographic entry only. See also the
[Second Annual Report]({{ "/archive/paulding-county-experiment-farm-second-annual-report-mcintire/" | relative_url }})
and
[Economics of Farming on Paulding Soil]({{ "/archive/economics-of-farming-on-paulding-soil-blosser/" | relative_url }}).
