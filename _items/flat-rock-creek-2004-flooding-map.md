The title/index map for a series of flooding-documentation photographs
along Flat Rock Creek, taken June 15, 2004, prepared by County Engineer
Mark R. Stockman — the same county engineer credited with the 1996
Forder Bridge historical marker in this archive's
[historical markers gazetteer]({{ "/archive/historical-markers-index/" | relative_url }}).
Red dots trace the creek's path across Paulding, Emerald, Jackson, and
neighboring townships, marking where photographs were taken. The
individual photographs themselves are not included here — only this
index map.
