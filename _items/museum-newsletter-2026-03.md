Covers the museum's winter kitchen remodel (largely completed with help
from Vantage Vocational School students), the upcoming Ohio Local History
Alliance Region 1 meeting hosted at the museum, the 2026 Spring Tea, and
the 2025 Festival of Trees recap.

The standout feature has been transcribed as its own entry in this
archive:

- ["Jesse B. Jackson: Paulding's Consul Who Saved Thousands"]({{ "/archive/jesse-b-jackson/" | relative_url }}) — the story of a Paulding, Ohio native who, as U.S. Consul in Aleppo, Syria beginning in 1908, is credited with saving thousands of Armenian lives during the Armenian Genocide, and how a chance phone call reunited his family's artifacts with the museum

See the full PDF for photos and a poem, "Paulding County," reprinted from
an undated *Garden Spot of Ohio* pamphlet.
