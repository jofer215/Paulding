A 1926 letter, a 1976 canal-town profile, and a whole run of 1990s local
newspaper features — this compilation gathers previously published
*Paulding County Progress* historical articles into one $2 booklet, "so
that once an article has been published, it doesn't become next to
impossible to retrieve," in editor Melinda Krick's words. It was intended
as the first in an ongoing series (Volume 2 was planned for January 2001).
